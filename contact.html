Dr. Elias Blum, M.D.

Dr. Blum is a specialist in heart-transplant surgery and a cardiothoracic fellow. Dr. Blum graduated magna cum laude from Harvard University and completed his residency at Johns Hopkins University. With over 20 years of experience in the medical field, Dr. Blum has a perfect success rate in all surgeries he has performed over the years, making him the youngest doctor to ever become Chief of Staff at Bellevue Hospital in New York City.
